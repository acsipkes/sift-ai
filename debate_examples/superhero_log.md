### Core Argument: The Necessity of the Mask (Initial Position: The_Outlaw_Legend)
*   **Premise:** Anonymity is a "private necessity" and a firewall against asymmetrical retaliation.
*   **Counter-Argument (Iron_Director):** Anonymity allows for total lack of recourse for victims and creates a class of citizens above the law.
*   **Synthesis:** Anonymity is preserved against the public and the state apparatus, but "escrowed" against the judicial process.

### Core Argument: Centralized Oversight
*   **Premise:** A central UN body ensures global standards and safety.
*   **Counter-Argument (The_Outlaw_Legend):** A central database is a "political collar" and a single point of failure for hackers or tyrants.
*   **Synthesis:** A decentralized, sharded registry where no single entity holds the "key" to an individual's identity, ensuring leverage-neutrality.
